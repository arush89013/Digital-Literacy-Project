# Social Media Etiquette Checklist
*Prepared by Arush Singh - Student Digital Ambassador*

### ✅ The Do's
* **Privacy First:** Regularly audit your privacy settings to manage who can access your profile information.
* **Professional Branding:** Use your platforms to showcase your academic achievements and tech projects.
* **Constructive Engagement:** Participate in healthy online discussions that add value to the community.
* **Verify Information:** Always fact-check news or articles before sharing them with your network.
* **Networking:** Follow and interact with industry experts to learn about the latest trends in CSE.

### ❌ The Don'ts
* **Offensive Content:** Avoid posting or sharing anything that could be seen as discriminatory or unprofessional.
* **Oversharing:** Never post sensitive data like your student ID, address, or financial details.
* **Online Conflict:** Refrain from engaging in heated arguments or using aggressive language in comments.
* **Unauthorized Tagging:** Don't post photos of peers or mentors without asking for their permission first.
* **Reputation Damage:** Avoid venting about your college or workplace publicly; keep professional grievances private.
