# 'Stay Safe Online' Prevention Guide

As a Student Digital Ambassador, I recommend these 8 actionable safety tips for college students:

1. **The Golden UPI Rule:** Never enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money. A PIN is only for *sending* or authorizing a debit. 
2. **Verify Merchant Names:** Before scanning a QR code at the canteen or local shops, confirm the name on your screen matches the actual merchant.
3. **Use Official Links:** Always type `cybercrime.gov.in` directly into your browser rather than clicking links in SMS or emails.
4. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Secure your VITyarthi and college email accounts with app-based 2FA. 
5. **Check for HTTPS:** Ensure the URL starts with `https://` and shows a padlock icon before entering any login credentials. 
6. **Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking:** Never use free railway or cafe Wi-Fi to access your bank account; use your mobile data instead.
7. **Report Immediately:** If you notice a fraudulent transaction, call the national helpline **1930** instantly to freeze the payment.
8. **Audit App Permissions:** Regularly check your phone settings to ensure apps don't have unnecessary access to your SMS (which contains OTPs).

## 🛡️ Emergency Reporting (India)
* **Helpline Number:** 1930 (Available 24x7)
* **Web Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
