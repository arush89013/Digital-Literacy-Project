# [cite_start]Task 5 Part B: 'Stay Safe Online' Prevention Guide [cite: 81]

[cite_start]As a Student Digital Ambassador, I recommend these 8 actionable safety tips for college students: [cite: 82]

1. **The Golden UPI Rule:** Never enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money. [cite_start]A PIN is only for *sending* or authorizing a debit. [cite: 83]
2. [cite_start]**Verify Merchant Names:** Before scanning a QR code at the canteen or local shops, confirm the name on your screen matches the actual merchant. [cite: 82]
3. [cite_start]**Use Official Links:** Always type `cybercrime.gov.in` directly into your browser rather than clicking links in SMS or emails. [cite: 84]
4. [cite_start]**Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Secure your VITyarthi and college email accounts with app-based 2FA. [cite: 82]
5. [cite_start]**Check for HTTPS:** Ensure the URL starts with `https://` and shows a padlock icon before entering any login credentials. [cite: 82]
6. [cite_start]**Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking:** Never use free railway or cafe Wi-Fi to access your bank account; use your mobile data instead. [cite: 82]
7. [cite_start]**Report Immediately:** If you notice a fraudulent transaction, call the national helpline **1930** instantly to freeze the payment. [cite: 84]
8. [cite_start]**Audit App Permissions:** Regularly check your phone settings to ensure apps don't have unnecessary access to your SMS (which contains OTPs). [cite: 82]
