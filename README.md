## 📁 Project Roadmap & Module Summaries
This repository is organized into five specialized tasks. Every tool used is free and browser-based, ensuring accessibility for all students.

### 🎨 [Task 1: Digital Awareness Infographic](./task-1-presentation/)
* **Focus:** Creating a visual guide on what digital literacy means and why it matters.
* **Content:** Covers safe internet practices and essential digital tools for first-year students.
* **Tools:** Canva

### 💼 [Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio](./task-2-portfolio/)
* **Focus:** Building a professional online presence.
* **Content:** Verified profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle**.
* **Goal:** Setting the foundation for internships and academic networking.

### 💻 [Task 3: Coding & Collaboration](./task-3-platforms/)
* **Focus:** Mastering technical and collaborative platforms.
* **Content:** A completed **HackerRank** challenge and a custom "Digital Literacy Quiz" via **Google Forms**.
* **Collaboration:** Demonstrating efficiency with Google Workspace.
* Quiz link-https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdQadkPSGCKzrx2P7MYazBjVo8_ogdTw0GaAbybpp-b7XtimA/viewform?

### 📧 [Task 4: Email Etiquette & Social Media](./task-4-email-etiquette/)
* **Focus:** Professional communication and responsible digital citizenship.
* **Content:** Formal email drafts for professors and internship coordinators, plus a **Social Media Do’s and Don’ts** checklist.

### 🔐 [Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness](./task-5-cybercrime/)
* **Focus:** Recognizing and avoiding online threats.
* **Content:** A deep-dive case study on **UPI/Payment Fraud** and an 8-step "Stay Safe Online" prevention guide.
* **Emergency:** Highlights the National Cyber Crime Helpline **1930**.
